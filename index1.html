about us

Welcome to Saafa Banquets,
one of the best banquet halls in Pune!

With its inception in June 2021 and built keeping luxury in mind, Saafa Banquets is a luxurious banquet hall service in Balewadi, Pune. The best part is that you can celebrate any kind of event at our plush banquet hall and open-air lawn. Having organised 150+ events, our impeccable services will uplift your event and make it a memorable one for everyone involved.

Whether it’s two people tying the knot, a corporate firm celebrating a great achievement, a person throwing a birthday bash, or any other event for that matter, everything is done in a grand manner.
